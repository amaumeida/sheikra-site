The Petunia Puzzle Garden

Welcome! Petunias are beautiful, trumpet-shaped flowering plants. In this game, 25 flowers are secretly planted in 5 pots (5 flowers per color: Red, Pink, White, Purple, Lilac). Your mission: recreate the exact arrangement!

How to Play: Click a pot, then click color buttons to plant. You can only use 5 flowers per color total, spread across all pots. Remove flowers by clicking them. Fill all pots to submit your guess!
